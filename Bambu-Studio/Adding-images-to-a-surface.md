# Adding images to a surface

* Convert the image to SVG: [https://pngtosvg.com/?v2](https://pngtosvg.com/?v2)
* Right click on object in Objects and go to `Add modifier -> SVG`
* Scale and place the SVG (depth should be .2 mm/layer)
